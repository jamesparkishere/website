Questions

Frequently asked.

Is the app free?

Yes — the app is completely free to download and use. There's never a charge to pre-qualify, calculate, or message James.

Does checking my pre-qualification hurt my credit score?

No. The in-app pre-qualification uses a soft, no-impact check on your self-reported information — nothing touches your credit score until you formally apply.

Is my information secure?

Yes. Personal information is encrypted in transit and at rest, the same standard used across the Canadian Mortgage App platform.

Does the app replace working with James directly?

Not at all — it's built to make working with James easier, not to replace him. Every file is still reviewed personally, and the in-app chat goes straight to him.

Where is it available?

Anywhere in BC — purchases, refinances, renewals, and specialized lending are all supported in-app.
