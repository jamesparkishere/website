Who we are

This site is operated by James Park, a licensed Mortgage Broker in British Columbia (License #503774), doing business as James Park MortgagePal. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, and how it's protected, in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and BC's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

Information we collect

Depending on how you use this site, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number
  • Financial information you provide for a pre-qualification estimate — income, debts, credit band, down payment, property details
  • Information you submit through contact forms, realtor/partner profile inquiries, or webinar/landing page registrations
  • Basic technical data (browser type, general location) collected automatically when you visit the site

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide a mortgage pre-qualification estimate and follow up with you personally
  • Connect you with a realtor, lawyer, notary, or other partner you request to be connected with
  • Send you account-related emails (certificates, login access, file status updates)
  • Respond to inquiries submitted through this site
  • Improve our services and this website

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except where necessary to provide the services you've requested (for example, connecting you with a partner realtor you've asked to be referred to), or where required by law.

How your information is stored

Your information is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider, with access controls restricting who can view your file to James Park and, where applicable, a partner you've been specifically connected with. We do not store full credit card or banking information on this site.

Cookies and local storage

This site uses browser local storage to keep you signed in and to remember basic preferences. We do not use third-party advertising trackers on this site at this time.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable privacy law.
